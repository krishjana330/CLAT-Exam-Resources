CLAT

The Common Law Admission Test (CLAT) is a centralized national level entrance test for admissions to twenty-two National Law Universities in India. Most private and self-financed law schools in India also use these scores for law admissions. The test is taken after the Higher Secondary Examination or the 12th grade for admission to integrated undergraduation programs in Law and after Graduation in Law for Master of Laws (LL.M) programs offered by these law schools.

Purpose of the Website

This website serves as a comprehensive resource for aspiring law students preparing for the CLAT exams. It offers access to a wealth of past examination papers spanning from 2008 to 2024. These resources are intended to help students familiarize themselves with the exam format, question types, and the breadth of topics covered in the exams, enhancing their preparation strategies.

By providing these resources, our aim is to empower students with the tools they need to succeed in their law entrance tests, thereby fostering a better prepared, more confident cohort of future law professionals.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide comprehensive, accessible, and up-to-date resources for students preparing for the Common Law Admission Test (CLAT). We aim to support aspiring law students by offering a platform where they can find all necessary materials to prepare effectively for their law entrance exams.
